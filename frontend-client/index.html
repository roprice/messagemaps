The more you write, the better the results

You're about to do your first interview. Interviews probe your brand, your competitors, your customers, your value propositions, and more. Your answers help create a brand strategy brief and actual messaging assets.

Later you'll be able to add additional interviews, upload audio transcripts, and add market research.

But first, we need to hear from you, in your own words. The more you write the better the results. So, let's get started.

brand nickname
How do you want to refer to it in your marketing? Consider how your team and customers refer to the brand
Imagine two people meet at a conference. One is your former customer. Your brand comes up when the other one asks for recommendations in your field. What does your former customer say about your brand versus your competitors?
Imagine the same scenario - what exactly do you want them to say?
Think about your ideal customers in terms of who benefits the most from what your brand offers. Why do they get more value from what you offer than other customers?
Who is similar to your ideal customer but wouldn't actually benefit from it? Who might regret buying it?
Imagine the ideal customer just before they hear your message - what are they thinking?
Ideally, what do they think after they hear your message?
Ideally, what action do they take after they hear your message?
